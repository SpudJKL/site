Our Bread

We make our award-winning bread daily in our artisan bakeries, using traditional methods, the highest quality ingredients & absolutely nothing artificial.

Just flour, water, sea salt and nothing else.

Rubens Bakery. It'll blow your mind.

The bakery was founded by expert baker, Ruben Lopez using his “special” sourdough recipe, "magicical" gluten free recipe, and fermentation process. Ruben’s passion and craft is still reflected in every loaf we bake.

Our Sourdough Class.

is lovingly made in our store every day using traditional methods. We still hand mix to this day and use the highest-grade ingredients from trusted partners including Kells flour and the finest Mediterranean sea salt.

Gluten Free bread. To die for.

Made from simple ingredients for a unique and delicious texture and taste. Unlike other Gluten free breads, there are no artificials, preservatives or additives.

At Rubens Bakery

we believe that everyone should have access to great quality gluten free and sourdough bread, every day. Bread that is free from additives and preservatives. Gluten free and sourdough bread that not only tastes delicious but is also nutritious and made with simple, natural ingredients by people that bake with care and attention.
